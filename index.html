About Me

Hi! My name's Brooke! I graduated from Penn State University with my Bachelor of Design in the spring of 2018. I was a part of the Interdisciplinary Digital Studio program at Penn State. While in the program I was able to learn all about digital design - I worked on projects of all kinds from 2D design, 3D design, interactive design, photography, photo editing, videography, video editing, physical computing, and game design.

After briefly searching for design jobs, I've decided to pursue web development to build off of my current skillset as a digital designer and open up more fulfilling career opportunities. I've always loved exploring new ways to create things and I'm so excited to be jumping into web development!

I currently work at Chipotle to make some $$$ on the side. When I'm not rolling burritos or coding you can usually find me playing guitar, sitting by the pool, binge watching TV, or playing video games.
